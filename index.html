About MJC

Mahjong Coach (MJC) is a learning tool for beginning and intermediate Mahjong players.

Using Six Box Theory™, MJC helps players learn, understand, and improve their Mahjong playing skills.

Additional coaching features arrive in Version 2.0.

Welcome to Mahjong Coach (MJC)

To get started:

  1. Select your Display preferences. This is the order in which you normally organize tiles on your rack.
  2. Select your Role: Player or Dealer.
  3. Select your Seat and Round winds.
  4. Enter your starting tiles.
  5. Review your settings and tile entries.
  6. Press Play On!

About 6BT

Six Box Theory™ (6BT) provides a method for understanding Mahjong hand structure.

Rather than viewing a hand as individual tiles, 6BT organizes it into boxes, offering a simpler way to visualize, understand, and develop your hand.

This makes it easier to recognize strengths and weaknesses, improve tile efficiency, and make better discard decisions.
